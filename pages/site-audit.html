A curated list of links that helped me a long the way.

1. Define Goals

We will analyze your products design effectiveness through an expert perspective and analysis of data. From these insights we will create a compelling and easy to understand document that can be shared internally or with other agencies. This document will define problem areas and practical actions that can be taken to increase usability. We will also explore a potential roadmap and methodology for validating changes.

Examples

  • Sales Page: How can we increase conversions.
  • Registration/Trail Process: Is this easy to complete, is it easy to upgrade to a full account.
  • App/Web: Can we make it easier for users to achieve their goals on your product.
  • Is the overall experience cohesive and on brand.

2. Collect Insights

a. Heuristic Audit - Detailed examination of product interface page by page against best practices. This could include but not limited to:

  • Registration Funnel ( e.g. Signup, Free Trail to Full Customer )
  • Product Details
  • Pricing Breakdown
  • App Interface
  • Mobile Experience
  • Navigation
  • Blog Section
  • Tone / Message / Audience / Consistency

b. Analytics Audit

  • Collection of Conversion Metrics, Bounce Rate, Time on page, Referring pages… etc.
  • Identify what data is being collected and what should be collected moving forward.
    • c. Internal Audit - Optional
  • Create and organize an internal survey meant to gather information from existing team members and decision makers.
    • d. Customer Audit - Optional
  • Create and organize a public survey meant to gather information from existing customers.

3. Organization and Analysis of Data

  • Creating Diagrams
  • Documenting Screenshots
  • Comparative Examples in the wild

4. Documentation of Findings

As we form hypothesis and opinions we will hold recomendations to certain core principles.

  • Relevance
  • Value proposition
  • Actionable

5. Validation of Ideas

Examples

  • A/B Testing
  • Increased Metrics
  • Customized Tracking
  • User Surveys

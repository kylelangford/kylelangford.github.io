SVG Animation (lottiefiles)

Ask Enel Green Power had an old scrolling animation that they wanted to update. The script was verbose and the style no longer matched the modern brand. This project was open ended but ultimatly the end result would go on a landing page and link out to other sections.

Outcome The content needed a major adaptation in order to fit the layout I envisioned. Adam and I worked with Enel to restructure and boil down the content in sections we defined in our content strategy. From there we storyboarded the scenes and animations. The animated SVG were contracted by a lottie artist. Major contributions were creative direction, animation direction, and implementation.

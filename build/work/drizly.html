Drizly

Application Design and Development

Standardizing Visual Language

Ask We had been coding emails for Drizly for several months. I designed a monthly calendar email and regularly built and designed HTML emails with the help of my partner Rachel Sorkin. In order to help them lower cost and streamline the email design process we proposed a component based design system and application to build, store and generate the HTML.

Email designs produced by: Kyle Langford and Rachel Sorkin

Outcome There were a lot of commonalities in the emails we built. Rachel and I consolidated their emails into a library of a dozen or so reusable templates. I coded and tested the components before integrating them with our system. I designed and built a UI for arranging these templates and populating them with content. This app would render the HTML in a preview panel in real time and also allow you to export the generated HTML, which we would place into Litmus and eventually into ExactTarget.

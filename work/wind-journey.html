Creative Direction and Development

Creative Technology

Ask Enel Green Power had an old scrolling flash movie that they wanted to make modern. The script was verbose and the style no longer matched the current branding. This project was open ended and relied on us to provide direction.

ContributionThe content needed a major adaptation in order to work on a webpage. My biggest contribution for this project is a strong vision for how we could adapt the content and use animation and interaction. I was able to quickly research and provide a concept for us to move forward with.

Outcome Adam and I worked with Enel to restructure and boil down the content in sections we defined in our content strategy. From there we storyboarded the scenes and animations. The animated SVG was contracted by a lottie artist. Major contributions were creative direction, animation direction, and implementation.

Introduction

A curated list of links that helped me a long the way.

My name is Kyle Langford. As an Interactive Designer I am an essential part of any project or product team. I combine a strong front end development skillset with a talent for crafting great UI designs. I have an aspiring interest in understanding more about the User Experiences. I am involved in the strategic / research aspects of projects and provide quick prototyping and wireframing capabilities. This helps teams move from sketches direct to code – good code – efficiently.

I work well sketching with team members and building prototypes and proofs of concepts. I like to focus on whether the interactions, process, flow, and the overall experience of the product working well for the end user.

As a leader and mentor I have experience managing expectations, communicating ideas and developing team strategies. I make sure to ask the right questions: is the product intuitive, easy to use and, ultimately, does it meet the user’s goals and expectations?

As a designer I want to share ownership of the UX, I enjoy working on defining the product, the audience, and conducting research. Being involved early allows me to understand the rationale behind the product and design approach and helps me to make good, consistent, UX decisions without needing every piece of UI defined.

As a developer I produce standards driven, production quality code. I have experience on building content heavy websites and theming Drupal and Shopify themes.
